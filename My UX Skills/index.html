My UX Skills

User Research

My approach to user research is that I want to understand the users. I want to find how to make their experiences last longer, and enjoyable enough to come back. I also what there to be a narrative and identifying what user need and want when using your website.

User Empathy

User empathy is that I want to really get into the shoes of the user. I want to know what they experience. I also want to know what the shortcomings are of their experiences and ways to make it easier for them and better.

Qualitative Interviews

When doing qualitative interviews, I try to dig deep into the users' experience and always try to ask specific questions to really capture the users' experience, likes and dislikes, in order to streamline it and make it a more enjoyable experience.
